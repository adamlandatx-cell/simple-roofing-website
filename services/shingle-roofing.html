Shingle Roofing FAQs

How long do shingle roofs last in Houston?

Quality architectural shingles typically last 15-25 years in Houston's climate with proper installation and ventilation.

Why Tamko instead of GAF or Owens Corning?

All three make good shingles. We chose Tamko for consistent quality, excellent adhesive performance, and better value.

Are impact-resistant shingles worth the extra cost?

Often yes, in Houston. Check with your insurance company about Class 4 discounts — they typically pay for themselves over time.
