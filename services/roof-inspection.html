What We Check During an Inspection

Shingle Condition

We look for curling, cracking, blistering, and granule loss — all signs of age and wear.

Flashing & Seals

Every penetration (vents, pipes, chimney, skylights) is a potential leak point.

Pipe Boots

Rubber pipe boots crack and fail before shingles do. We check every boot.

Ridge & Hip Caps

These take the most wind abuse. We check for lifted, cracked, or missing cap shingles.

Gutters & Drainage

Clogged or damaged gutters cause water to back up under shingles.

Ventilation

Proper attic ventilation extends roof life. We check that vents are functioning.

Decking (Where Visible)

From the attic, we can spot soft spots, water stains, or daylight peeking through.

Fascia & Soffit

Water damage often shows up at the roof's edge first.
