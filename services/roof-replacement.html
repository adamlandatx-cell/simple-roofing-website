Roof Replacement in Houston

Planned upgrades for homeowners who want it done right — not storm chasers looking for a quick sale.

Simple Roofing Co specializes in planned roof replacements for Houston homeowners. We're not chasing storms or pushing insurance claims. We work with homeowners who know it's time for a new roof and want a contractor they can trust to do it right.

How We Replace Your Roof

1

Free Inspection & Consultation

We assess your current roof, discuss your goals, and explain your options. No obligation, no pressure tactics.

2

Transparent Quote

You receive a detailed Cost Plus estimate breaking down materials, labor, and our markup. You see exactly what you're paying for.

Material Selection

Choose from our Tamko shingle lines — Heritage, Storm Fighter Flex, or Titan XT. We'll help you pick the right color and style.

Professional Installation
