What Does a Roof Replacement Cost in Houston?

Every roof is different. Size, pitch, accessibility, decking condition, and material choice all affect the final price. That said, here's what drives costs:

  • Roof size — Measured in "squares" (100 sq ft each). Average Houston home: 25-35 squares.
  • Material choice — Heritage vs Storm Fighter Flex vs Titan XT affects material cost.
  • Tear-off layers — One layer vs two affects labor time.
  • Decking repairs — Rotted or damaged decking must be replaced.
  • Roof complexity — Valleys, dormers, and steep pitches add labor.
  • Ventilation upgrades — Ridge vents, soffit vents, or power ventilators if needed.

Roof Replacement FAQs

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most Houston roof replacements take 1-3 days depending on size and complexity. Weather can extend this — we won't install in rain or high winds.

Do I need to be home during the replacement?

No. Many homeowners leave for work and come back to a new roof. We'll call you for the final walkthrough.

What warranty do I get?

You get Tamko's manufacturer warranty on materials plus our workmanship warranty covering installation.

Do you work with insurance claims?

We focus on planned replacements, not insurance work. If you have storm damage, we can inspect and document it, but we don't chase claims or negotiate with adjusters.

What is Cost Plus pricing?

Cost Plus means we show you the actual cost of materials and labor, then add a fixed markup percentage. No hidden fees, no inflated line items.
