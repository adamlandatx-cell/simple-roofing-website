What the Homeowner Wanted

This Greatwood homeowner had a clear vision: they wanted the best roof available and were willing to pay for it. Their custom-built home was approaching 20 years old, and rather than just match what they had, they wanted an upgrade — something that would stand out in the neighborhood and last as long as they planned to stay.

Our Solution

We recommended Tamko Titan XT in Aged Wood — our top-tier shingle with enhanced thickness, superior granule adhesion, and a 150 MPH wind warranty.

The scope included:

  • Complete tear-off of existing roof system
  • Full synthetic underlayment with ice & water shield in all valleys
  • New pipe boots, flashing, and drip edge throughout
  • Tamko Titan XT shingles with color-matched hip and ridge caps
  • Solar-powered attic fan for improved ventilation

The complex roofline — multiple gables, dormers, and seven valleys — required precise installation. This is where crew experience matters.
