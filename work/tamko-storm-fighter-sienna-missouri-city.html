What the Homeowner Wanted

This Sienna homeowner's roof was 16 years old — not failing, but past its prime. Several neighbors had filed hail claims recently. Rather than wait for their luck to run out, they wanted to be proactive.

Their question: "Should we just replace with what we have, or upgrade to something more hail-resistant?"

Our Solution

We helped them calculate the break-even point. Their insurer confirmed a 19% discount for a Class 4 roof. The Storm Fighter Flex would "pay for itself" in approximately 8 years through premium savings — and they'd likely be in the home another 15-20 years.

We installed Tamko Storm Fighter Flex in Rustic Cedar. The scope included:

  • Complete tear-off of original builder-grade shingles
  • Decking inspection (solid throughout)
  • Synthetic underlayment with ice & water shield at eaves and valleys
  • Tamko Storm Fighter Flex shingles with Class 4 impact rating
  • Conversion from box vents to continuous ridge vent
  • All new pipe boots
