What the Homeowner Wanted

This Bunker Hill Village homeowner was tired of dealing with their roof after every major storm. They'd been through two insurance claims in the past decade for hail damage. When the third storm left visible dents, they decided to break the cycle.

Their requirement: "What's the most storm-resistant option you install?"

Our Solution

We recommended Tamko Storm Fighter Flex in Weathered Wood — Class 4 impact-resistant with enhanced wind performance. Their insurer confirmed a 22% discount on dwelling coverage for a Class 4 roof.

The scope included:

  • Complete tear-off of existing hail-damaged system
  • Full decking inspection (fortunately solid)
  • Synthetic underlayment with ice & water shield at all critical points
  • Tamko Storm Fighter Flex throughout (approximately 48 squares)
  • Comprehensive ridge vent system

The insurance discount will total approximately $4,000 over the roof's expected life. The upgrade essentially pays for itself while providing better protection.
