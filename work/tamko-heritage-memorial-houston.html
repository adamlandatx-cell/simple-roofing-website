What the Homeowner Wanted

This Memorial homeowner had been putting off the roof for years. They bought the home in 2008 knowing the roof was already 15 years old. "We'll deal with it eventually" turned into 17 more years of patches and crossed fingers during storms.

A small leak in the master bedroom closet finally forced the decision. They wanted it done right, once, so they could stop thinking about the roof entirely.

Our Solution

The inspection confirmed what they feared: this roof was living on borrowed time. Multiple pipe boots had failed completely. The chimney flashing had separated from the masonry.

We recommended Tamko Heritage in Weathered Wood. The scope included:

  • Complete tear-off revealing original 1990s decking
  • Replacement of 14 sheets of OSB decking
  • Complete chimney re-flash with new step and counter flashing
  • All new pipe boots, vent flashing, and drip edge
  • Tamko Heritage shingles throughout
  • Continuous ridge vent replacing original box vents
  • Gutter guards installed (homeowner add-on)
